My Journey So Far

Blending engineering precision with the art of documentation — this is my story and the path I’m shaping ahead.

Where It Began

My name is Shishir S Tambe — a Civil Engineer by education, a Technical Consultant by profession, and a Writer by evolution.

It all began with a moment that shook a city — the Elphinstone Road stampede in 2017. I wasn’t there, but that event triggered something in me. It revealed the consequences of poor infrastructure and inspired me to do something about it.

The Shift

After years of studying and working in infrastructure, I discovered my strength wasn’t just in executing systems — but in explaining them. That realization pushed me toward technical writing.

From writing detailed civil engineering reports to contributing documentation in open-source projects, I found my new voice. Now, I bring clarity to complexity — whether in concrete or code.

“I want to build things that outlive me — in both code and concrete.”
